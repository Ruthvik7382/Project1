Different formats in cricket

These days cricket got many formats but mainly there are just three formats

  • Test Match
  • One Day International
  • T20

A brief explanation on these formats

Test match is a 5 day game and will be played mostly in daylight i.e from 9Am to 5pm and this time varies with respect to climate. One Day International as the name suggests will be played within a day and is limited over game comprising of 50 overs per side. T20 is the shortest format in the cricket game which have 20 overs per side to play.
